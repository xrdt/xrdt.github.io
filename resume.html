Positions I am Interested in

  • Ones where I get to work with good people
  • Ones where I can see the Quality in my work

I think the most important predictor of how well an experience goes is how you judge the quality of the people you're sharing the experience with. Counting grains of sand on the beach in 100% humidity and over 100 deg Fahrenheit weather can be enjoyable with the right group of people. It's a hard thing to determine who is good, but it doesn't have to be hard to stick around those people or keep those people around.

I can't say I fully understand my second criterion, because I don't fully understand Pirsig's Metaphysics of Quality, but I do want to develop a certain kind of passion for and dedication to my "craft", whatever it ends up being.

The most fundamental criterion for having a good life is to want to be alive.
